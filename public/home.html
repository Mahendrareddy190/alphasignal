Under the hood
How signals are generated
A 2-of-3 majority vote across three independent indicators keeps signals high-confidence and filters out noise.
📡
Live Data Stream
Live WebSocket pushes real-time candle updates across all 220 pairs simultaneously. Chart refreshes every 400 ms.
⚙️
Indicator Computation
RSI(14), MACD(12,26,9), EMA9/21, and Supertrend(10,3) are computed on every candle close from live data.

Consensus Vote
2-of-3 indicators must agree. BUY when 2+ bullish, SELL when 2+ bearish, HOLD otherwise. Confidence 0–100% shown live.

BUY · SELL · HOLD

Each indicator casts a vote. Only when two or more agree does a signal fire — eliminating single-indicator false positives.
